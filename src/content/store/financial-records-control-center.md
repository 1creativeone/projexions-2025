## What it is
A monthly “close-lite” routine that prevents the cycle of falling behind and doing annual cleanup.

## What you get
- A monthly checklist you can repeat
- A recon tracker by account
- An anomalies/exceptions log so nothing depends on memory
- A doc map + CPA handoff view when needed

## Who it’s for
Businesses that want to stay stable year-round without overbuilding a system.

## Not for
Full dashboards, forecasting suites, or advanced analytics.
