## What it is
A readiness check that identifies what will slow down (or block) tax prep and professional review.

## What you get
- A readiness rating: **Ready / Nearly Ready / Not Ready / High Risk**
- A short list of **specific blockers** to fix first
- A Notion “hub” to gather links, notes, and exceptions in one place

## Who it’s for
Anyone preparing for tax filing, financing, or CPA review.

## Not for
A substitute for a CPA or tax advice.
